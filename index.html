Our Story

A family business with one objective.

Love My Puppy was founded to fill a need in the Boca Raton community — a place where finding your next family member feels personal, not transactional.

We take pride in keeping impeccably clean facilities that reflect our upscale standards, as well as our personal affection for every pup. We sell puppies, groom, sell accessories, and offer boarding — everything under one roof.

A wide selection of toys, grooming accessories, bedding, and apparel are regularly stocked. Special requests and orders are always welcome.
